Will H Curry Development & Design

I develop and design websites and web applications with style and functionality with an emphasis on pleasantries of the user's experience. You should feel the ease of the browse as you navigate. Your fingers and eyes should tell your brain you are happy when a site responds quickly and beautifully. You shouldn't have to dread surfing on a phone or tablet. Skills

Programming

Good applications come from good code. Good code comes from good programming practices. My programming language proficiencies include JavaScript(JQuery), HTML5, CSS3, PHP5, XML, and C. I'm also experienced with C#, Razor, and Visual Basic.

Software

Using development environments like JetBrains PhpStorm and Microsoft Visual Studio, editing suites like Adobe Photoshop, and my favorite text editor Sublime Text, I'm learning to master skills such as MVC, ASP.NET, AJAX, JSon, and LESS.

Databases

Although I'm primarily a front-end specialist, I also do back-end to keep my sites connected to the cloud using PHP. My current study focuses are databases and DBMS (DataBase Management Systems). SQL, MySQL, and phpMyAdmin will soon become strengths.

Operating Systems

I currently create most of my work on the wonderful Windows 10 and Ubuntu Linux. Other operating systems I practice are Windows XP/Vista/7/8.1 and Red Hat Enterprise Linux.

Mobile Friendly

Using mobile friendly techniques and frameworks you can be guaranteed that each of my websites are search-engine-optimized and will look just as good on your mobile phone as they do on your big screen HDTV.

And Beyond...
